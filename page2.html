Protecting your security online
  • Keep Your computer up to date with anti-virus firewall software
  • Do not leave your computer unattended while logged in to HNB
    Virtual branch
  • Close the browser window you used to login to virtual branch
    after signing out


For security purposes if the login screen or any other screen within Virtual Branch is
left unattended for more than 5 minutes, you will be automatically logged off and
will be required to login again.
